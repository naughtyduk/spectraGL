Nebula

In the depths of space, where there should be nothing, there is something.

Matter coalesces from the void, light bends around the unseen, and time itself becomes a question rather than an answer. We are stardust contemplating stardust, consciousness emerging from chaos to witness its own becoming.

This is where we began.
